Welcome to The Focus Blend!

The Focus Blend first opened it's doors in April of 2026 with a mission in mind. Not only do we consider ourselves a coffee shop; we also strive to be a unique space for students and adults alike to focus. In order to accommodate different types of work styles, we are open 24 hours and offer a variety of themed study sessions on a weekly basis. Whether you're here for mocha or math, welcome to The Focus Blend!

How We Came to Be

The Focus Blend was founded by Dr. Cheyanne Lynn, an associate professor of business and culinary arts at Juniata College. While teaching, she saw a need for a study location that was easily accessible to students of all learning styles, available at all times, and provided some distance from the college itself. Thus, an idea was born!

The Focus Blend perfectly pairs relaxation and comfort with productivity. The Pretzel Caramel Latte doesn't hurt either!

- Julia W, Juniata College Student
